London

September 2-8, 2024

The summer before my senior year of undergrad, I went to London. This trip was my semi-impulsive, first international trip without my parents. My sister and I had been talking about traveling together for years, and after realizing that I didn't know where I would be after graduation, we thought that it was now or never to take this opportunity. This trip truly inspired what this website is all about!

Day 0

Flight and First Time on the Tube!

About to Take Off to London!
Taking a Break at the Airport
My Sister Taking a Nap on the Tube

After a 6 hour red-eye flight from Boston to London, we arrived in London-Heathrow Airport. After being so exhaused from travelling, my sister took a break in the airport chairs before figuring out how to ride the London Tube for the first time. It was surprisingly easy and very reliable. My sister and I took turns napping and clutching onto our luggage during the hour-long train ride.

Exploring Westminster Area

Buckingham Palace!
0.5 in Front of Buckingham Palace
My First (But Not Last) Time Seeing the Tower of London

We checked into our hotel and ended up taking a crazy long nap. We woke up around 5 PM and explored to see Buckingham Palace, the London Eye, and Big Ben.

Dinner

Restaurant Ambiance at Cinnamon Kitchen
Our Appetizers: Samosa Chaat, Smoked Sweet Potato Cake, and Indo-Chinese Chilli Paneer
Our Entrees: Roast Porotobello Mushrooms and Lucknowi Chicken Biryani

We ended the night at a restaurant in Battersea Power Station called Cinammon Kitchen. It was my first time trying Indian food and I loved it. The owner was very nice and the food was very accommodating to my allergies. My sister and I still talk about this place on a regular basis.

Day 1

Morning Coffee

Morning Coffee With My Sister
My Coffee Latte 8/10

For our first full day in London, we started our morning off with coffee at Arabica in Convent Garden. I had a simple Coffee Latte and my sister got a Spanish Latte.

Tower Bridge

The Iconic Tower Bridge
Sitting on the Glass Floor Above the Bridge
Views From the Tower Bridge

Although its misconception to being the London Bridge, we visited the iconic Tower Bridge where we got to walk on the glass bridge connecting the two towers. It was cool to be in one of the most famous landmarks in London and having aerial views of the city and the Thames River.

Lunch

Borough Market
Pulled Pork Sandwiches
(Pre) Chocolate Covered Strawberries from Borough Market

We had lunch at Borough Market, one of the oldest and largest food markets in London. There were so many options to choose from, but one of the most famous places to eat at the market is the pulled pork sandwich stand called "The Black Pig." We also tried the famous chocolate covered strawberries and walked around to see all the different food stalls. Overall, it was a great experience with many food options to choose from.

Sky Garden

Sisters at Sky Garden
Leadenhall Market

After lunch, we went to Sky Garden, a public garden located at the top of the "Walkie Talkie" building. The views of London from up there were incredible, and we took lots of photos with the city skyline in the background. On our way back, we stopped by Leadenhall Market, a beautiful covered market with shops and restaurants. This was also where some scenes from the Harry Potter movies were filmed!
