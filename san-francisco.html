San Francisco

November 8-11, 2024

San Francisco was a quick long weekend trip with my sister and unexpectedly some of my family I haven't seen in many years. I actually went to San Francisco for a concert but it ended up being one of my shortest and most hectic trips because of everything I did. Try to guess who I went to see before it's revealed at the end of this post!

Day 0

Flight and Family Meetup!

So Much Leg Room on the Plane
Hi Mt. Rainier!
My Uber Driver AKA My Mom Picking Me Up at the Airport

I flew from Seattle to San Francisco on Friday. Since this was a relatively short flight, the plane was a bit smaller and since I was sitting in the exit row, I had so much leg room! Before fully leaving Seattle, I got to see Mt. Rainier which is pretty rare since the sky needs to be very clear to see it. After landing, my mom was gracious enough to pick me up and drive me to the hotel my sister and I were staying at. Coincidentally, she was visiting her sisters and mom in. Sacramento that same weekend so we got to meet up. We ate some Chipotle, caught up, and waited for my sister to arrive from her flight.

Caffeine Fix

Karma Coffee
Sister Exploring San Francisco
A Cool Parking Enforcement Car Art Piece Outside of the Coffee Shop

The next day, my sister and I explored a bit of San Francisco before our concert that night. We first got coffee at a local coffee shop called Karma Coffee. The interior was very unique and vibrant which I felt reflected the aesthetic of San Francisco

Haight-Ashbury Vintage Shopping

Selfie in a Vintage Shop
Photomatica in San Francisco
Our photobooth strips!
My first time seeing a Waymo
Tryng on Vintage Christmas Sweaters
Our Tacos: Carne Asada and Al Pastor

After our caffeine fix, we walked around Haight-Ashbury, the famous neighborhood known for its history with the 1960s counterculture movement and unique shops. We walked around and explored many vintage stores. My favorite store was this shop that had a variety of different vintage photobooths. My sister and I spent way too much money but I love to collect photobooth strips from different places I visit. We also stopped by a taco shop for a quick lunch.

Best Boba of My Life and Iconic Buildings

Best Boba of My Life
The Painted Ladies
Our failed attempt at a heart in our shadow

After lunch, we tried a popular boba chain called Boba Guys and it was honestly the best boba I've ever had in my life. I got my usual mango green tea and would buy again in a heartbeat. The boba shop was close to the iconic Painted Ladies houses so we walked over there to take some pictures. I love the architecture of these houses and the vibrant colors of the city.

Sabrina Carpenter in San Francisco!

Sold Out Show in San Francisco!
Sabrina Singing Dumb & Poetic
Sabrina Singing Sharpest Tool

We shuttled back to the hotel to get ready for the concert that night. We went to see Sabrina Carpenter live at Crypto Arena! (Did you guess right?) I've been a fan of her since I was young so it was great to be able to see one of my favorite artists It was such a fun experience seeing her live and dancing along to all her songs. My favorite song she sang was Sharpest Tool where she had a bathroom set that had a mirror connecting to a camera projected on the screen.
